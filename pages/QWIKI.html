BranD 73; Layout Designs That Change Reading, Sendpoints Publishing Co., Limited, Hongkong

same segments changing space same existence changing reality is a poster about identity and space. The poster visualizes the change in one's own identity when moving, fleeing, etc. The parts in the upper and lower half of the poster are identical, only their arrangement changes. This creates two people from the same parts, but with a different appearance. The area of each person corresponds to exactly one quarter of the total area of the poster. All parts and the background have a black or white proportion of exactly 50%, which, if the colors were separated, would result in a poster that is half black and half white. The poster has been sold at an exhibition in Spazio Alelaie to raise funds for humanitarian aid in the Gaza Strip.
